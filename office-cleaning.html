Commerical Cleaning Services

    From small offices to large premises we can provide a tailor made package to suit your business and budget. Our fully vetted, hardworking and reliable staff will guarantee an unrivalled level of service. We cover:

    • Tailor made packages
    • Regular bin changing and cleaning
    • Dusting and polishing of desks, equipment and rooms
    • Toilet cleaning
    • Kitchen cleaning
    • Windows and doors
    • Fully insured, vetted staff
    • Extensive monitoring for quality assurance

    We can add any other requirements to your package, just speak to one of our friendly staff who will tailor the service to your individual needs.
